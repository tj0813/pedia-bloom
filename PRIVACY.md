# Privacy Policy — Pedia Bloom

**Last updated:** 2026-06-10 · Applies to the app at https://tj0813.github.io/pedia-bloom/

Pedia Bloom is a children's educational app, and it is built to be **private by
construction**. The short version: **nothing you do in the app ever leaves your device.**

## What we collect
**Nothing is sent anywhere.** The app has no backend, no server, and no account system.
The only data it stores is your learning progress, kept **locally on your own device** in
the browser's `localStorage`:

- which topics have been opened (to power the streak, badges, level/XP, and the parent dashboard);
- your accessibility preferences (dark mode, dyslexia-friendly font, larger text);
- the explorer name you optionally type in Settings.

This data never reaches us or any third party. You can erase all of it at any time from
**Mode Orang Tua → Reset Data Belajar**, or by clearing the site's browser data.

## What we do NOT do
- ❌ No analytics, tracking, cookies, or fingerprinting.
- ❌ No third-party or advertising requests — the app makes **zero external network calls**
  (fonts, styles, and images are all self-hosted; verified in the test suite).
- ❌ No accounts, logins, email, or personal information collected.
- ❌ No advertising and no child-to-child messaging or social features.
- ❌ No location, camera, microphone, or contacts access.

## Children's privacy
Because the app collects no personal information and transmits nothing off-device, it is
designed to align with children's privacy principles (e.g. COPPA / GDPR-K) by default. The
"Tanya AI" feature is a **scripted, offline preview** — it has no live model and sends no
input anywhere.

## Hosting
The app is served as static files by GitHub Pages. GitHub may process standard server request
logs (e.g. IP address) as part of serving any website; see GitHub's privacy statement. The
application itself adds no tracking on top of that.
